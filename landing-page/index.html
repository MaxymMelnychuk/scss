Here's how it works

1. Discovery

We start by learning about your growth strategy and the target criteria for deals that make a perfect fit.
To do that, an M&A expert from the SourceCo team will work with you to understand :

  • What are you looking for in a management team?
  • What is your growth plan, and how do acquisitions fit into that?
  • Is there a chronological plan for the markets you want to go after first?
  • How do you prefer to structure your deals, and where are you willing to negotiate?
  • And everything else that's relevant

2. Assign Your Team

Next, we use the details we learned in discovery to assign your team of best-in-class experts. Including:

  • Outreach experts
  • An account manager
  • Investisment bankers
  • Researchers
  • Copywriters

These are the people who are dedicated to your account so we can stack the deck in favor of its success.

3. Research, Data, and Target Profiles

Once your team is assigned, they calibrate Alfred (our proprietary software) with the details you provided about your ideal targets. then the system scrapes the internet to populate our data warehouse.
The result?

  • A new data source that sidesteps deals that other buy-side firms are competing over.
  • A selective list of opportunities that appear to meet your criteria.
  • And, a deep understanding of the people we'll contact.

4. Ultra-Personalized Online-to-Offline Outreach

Next, we leverage the data and target profiles to build laser-targeted outreach campaigns that come directly from your branded domain.

We engage with companies that fit your investment criteria throught ultra-personalized messaging that resonates with senior-level executives. And our custom CRM orchestrates fully congruent compaigns throught Linkedin, email, sequences, hard-written direct mail, calls, and voicemail scripts.

SourceCo thrives on our mutual success

You can count on SourceCo to deliver the exact opportunities that fit into your strategic growth plan because our incentives are aligned. We make money when you close deals, so we'll never waste your time by scheduling bad opportunities.
